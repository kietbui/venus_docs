Aeon Labs Smart Energy Plug-In Dimmer¶

How to add to VENUS app¶

To add device to VENUS, below actions are required:

  • Press “Add button” (button ‘+’) in app

  • Reset device:

    Power-on, led will blink slowly

    Press Z-WAVE button

    –> If device is added successfully, led will stop blinking

  • Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and add it to VENUS

Configuration description¶

1. Signal Trigger Controller

  • Disable: Not send notification to controller when turning ON/OFF
  • Enable (Default): Send notification to controller when turning ON/OFF
