Smartenit ZigBee HA Metering Controller, Dual Load¶

How to add to VENUS app¶

Newly add device to Zigbee network
  • Press “Add button” (button ‘+’) in app
  • Plugin device to power-supply socket
  • The device Status LED will flash rapidly
  • Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app
  • As completing, the Status LED’s blinking rate will slow to once per second for 4 minutes
Reset and reconnect
  • Press “Add button” (button ‘+’) in app
  • Plugin device to power-supply socket
  • Press and hold the SET button for at least 20 seconds, then release
  • The Status LED will go out for 1 second and then flash rapidly
  • Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app
  • As completing, the Status LED’s blinking rate will slow to once per second for 4 minutes

Configuration description¶

There is no configuration for this device.
