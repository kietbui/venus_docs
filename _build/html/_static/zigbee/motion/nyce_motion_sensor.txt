Brief information
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	Nyce Controlâ€™s NCZ-3041 is a wireless motion sensor using the ZigBee radio protocol. It is used in conjunction with a low RF transmit power wireless ZigBee network to monitor the occupancy state of indoor areas, and provide this information to other devices operating in a ZigBee Home Automation network. 


How to add to VENUS app  
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Insert battery and then press Button once
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app
