Schlage RP200VZVNNSL - Z-Wave Plugin Dimmer Module
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

	.. image:: ../../images/switch_dimmer/schlage_plugin_dimmer_module.jpg
	.. :align: left
	

Brief information 
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	- Reduce energy use and save money by turning lights and small appliances off
	- Set to automatically trigger ''lights on'' with a Schlage Home Keypad Lock when you enter your code at the door
	- Expands the reach of Z-Wave in your home by extending its range up to 200 additional feet
	- This Z-Wave enabled product communicates wirelessly to other Z-Wave products
	- Works with incandescent table and floor lamps, use the Schlage Home Appliance Module for lamps with CFL bulbs
	
How to add to VENUS app  
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	**1. Activation**
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Plugin device to power socket
		- Double press program button in front of device
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app
	
	**2. Reset then re-add**
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Double press program button in front of device (to reset)
		- Double press program button in front of device once more (to add)
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app


Configuration description
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	There is no configuration in this device.
