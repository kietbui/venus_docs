Brief information 
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	- On/Off control via Z-Wave, manual wall switch and/or built-in button
	- Optimized antenna range
	- Fits in most gang boxes worldwide
	- Turns an existing wall switch into a Z-Wave switch
	- NOTE: Please read the product description to know specific instructions
	
How to add to VENUS app  
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	**1. Activation**
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Plugin device to power socket
		- Turn ON external switch or Press internal button
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app
	
	**2. Reset then re-add**
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Turn ON external switch 10 times in quick succession or Press internal button
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app
		
	.. image:: ../../images/switch_dimmer/aeon_inwall_micro_controller_s.png
	.. :align: left
	
		1. Live - Hot wire (Black wire) Connect to Live terminal of Micro Smart Energy Switch G2.
