Aeon Labs Aeotec Z-Wave LED Light Bulb, Gen5

Brief information
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	- Thousands of colors available, warm and cold white, adjustable, replaces 60W incandescent bulb
	- 5 color channels Red, Blue, Green, Warm White and Cool White
	- LEDs offer low power consumption and low heat generation. Fits into a regular E27 socket
	- Z-Wave Compatible, App available for iOS and Android
	- Easily make it part of your home automation system and control it from anywhere in the world
	- Compatible with Most Z-Wave Home Automation Hubs

How to add to VENUS app  
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	#. Newly add to VENUS network
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Turn the power to the bulb OFF then ON.
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app
	#. Reset device to add to new target VENUS network
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Turn the power to the bulb OFF then ON within 2 seconds and repeat 3 times.
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app

Configuration description
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	There is no configuration in this device.
