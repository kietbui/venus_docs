Basic operation  
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	- Multiple location on/off control when used with Z-wave enabled devices
	- Pre-set scene control when used with up to five Z-wave enabled devices
	- Full 229 device scene control when used with Intermatic InTouch server (CA5500B)
	- Wide detection pattern can be used for whole room coverage
	- Meets ASHRAE Standard 90.1 requirements
	
How to add to VENUS app  
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	**1. Activation**
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Insert battery
		- LED flashes red and white
		- Press front button twice
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app
	
	**2. Reset then re-add**
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Press front button twice (reset)
		- The LED starts flashing red and white
		- Press front button twice (re-add)
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app

How to add/remove associated device(s) to
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	**To add associated device(s) to this sensor, below action is required:**
	
	- Add z-wave notified-devices (which will be associated to this sensor) to VENUS
	- Select Associate button and then select notified-device(s) to add
	- Press and hold **front button** in few seconds
	- If successful, pop-up notification displays in VENUS app
	
	**To remove associated device(s) from this sensor, below action is required:**
	
	- Select Associate button and then select notified-device(s) to remove
	- Press and hold **front button** in few seconds
	- If successful, pop-up notification displays in VENUS app
