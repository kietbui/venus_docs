Telguard GDC1 - Universal Z-Wave Garage Door Control Switch
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

	.. image:: ../../images/door_window/telguard_garage_door_control_switch.jpg
	.. :align: left
	

Brief information 
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	- Is compatible with any Z-Wave controller supporting the binary switch command class. (Check with your controller dealer )
	- Meets UL 325 safety guidelines for unattended operation:Provides five seconds of audible and visual warnings prior to remote movement. Has the ability to cancel movement locally during the warning period. Suspends unattended operation after two consecutive failed closed attempts.
	- The GDC1 is based off one of the fundamental command classes in Z-Wave home automation, the binary switch (i.e. light switch). This means that the GDC1 will seamlessly integrate with nearly any Z-Wave based system ever sold that supports light switches. Turn the GDC1
	
How to add to VENUS app  
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	**1. Activation**
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Powerup device
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app
	
	**2. Reset then re-add**
		- Press "Add button" (button '+') in app
		- Press the z-wave button once (to reset)
		- Press the z-wave button once more (to add)
		- Wait for VENUS scan & detect this device and inform in app
